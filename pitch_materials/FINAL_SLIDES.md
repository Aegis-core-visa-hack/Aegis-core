> "ServiceNow tracks compliance tasks but won't detect a credit card number in your logs. AEGIS finds violations automatically, in real-time."

**Q: Can this replace compliance staff?**
> "Augment, not replace. AEGIS handles the 80% detectable by pattern. Humans handle judgment calls and strategy."

**Q: How do you handle false positives?**
> "Three-layer approach. Programmatic has near-zero false positives. Ambiguous cases go to human review. System learns from feedback."

**Q: What about data privacy?**
> "AEGIS processes metadata and patterns, not raw data. LLM analysis uses anonymized snippets. All configurable per organization."

**Q: Why Visa?**
> "Visa has unmatched PCI-DSS expertise and works with thousands of financial institutions facing this problem. Perfect partnership."

**Q: What's GenUI?**
> "Generative User Interface. Instead of static dashboards, AI generates custom visualizations for each query."
